## **About FinTeen**

FinTeen is a financial management platform designed specifically for the younger generation. We believe that financial literacy should start early, and we aim to make financial management easy and enjoyable.

With intuitive features and smart analytics, FinTeen helps you make better financial decisions, maintain financial stability, and achieve your savings goals more efficiently.

---

## **Why FinTeen**

FinTeen helps you manage your finances in a more structured way and understand potential risks before making decisions through financial simulations.

## **Key Features of FinTeen**

| Feature             | Description                                                             | Benefit                          |
|---------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Dashboard           | Provides an overall summary of your financial condition                 | Quickly monitor your finances    |
| Transactions        | Records daily income and expenses                                       | Control your cash flow           |
| Debt Simulation     | Calculates installments, interest, and loan risks                       | Avoid poor financial decisions   |
| Savings Target      | Create and track your savings progress                                  | Achieve financial goals          |
| Analytics & Insights| Provides analysis and spending patterns                                 | Support better decision-making   |

With these features, FinTeen becomes the ideal solution for young people to manage their finances wisely and achieve financial stability.

---

## **Target Users of FinTeen**

FinTeen is designed to help young individuals manage their finances more wisely and achieve financial stability.

### Main Target Users

1. **Students**  
   Helps students manage their allowance wisely and avoid overspending.

2. **University Students**  
   Assists in managing finances independently, whether living away from home or not.

3. **Young Professionals**  
   Helps manage income (salary), control expenses, and increase savings effectively.

With this wide range of users, FinTeen provides a solution to financial mismanagement caused by a lack of proper financial planning.
