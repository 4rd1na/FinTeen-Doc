## **Preparation Before Login**

Before logging in, make sure the following requirements are met to avoid any issues:

- Your FinTeen account is registered and verified via email  
- Your username and password are ready  
- Your device is connected to a stable internet connection  
- Your operating system meets the application requirements  

---

## **Logging into the FinTeen Platform**

This section is intended for users who already have a registered account and want to access the FinTeen system to use its features and services.

### Steps to Log In

1. Open the FinTeen platform  
2. Enter your username and password  
3. Click the **Login** button  
4. Wait for the authentication and data synchronization process  

If the login is successful, you will be redirected to the Profile Setup page to complete your initial data.

---

<Callout type="warn" title="Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)">
  - **Unable to log in even with correct credentials**  
    Make sure there are no mistakes in uppercase and lowercase letters in your username and password.  

  - **Platform cannot be accessed**  
    Try reopening the platform and ensure your system is compatible.  

  - **Forgot password**  
    Use the password reset feature on the official FinTeen website.  
</Callout>

<Tips>
## **Usage Tips**

If you experience issues while logging in, consider the following:

**Account Check**
- Recheck your username and password  
- Ensure your account is still active  
- Make sure your email has been verified  

**System Check**
- Ensure your internet connection is stable  
- Restart your device if necessary  

**Additional Solutions**
- Try logging in again after a few minutes  
- Ensure no firewall is blocking the platform  
- Contact support if the issue persists  

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you encounter problems during login, try the following steps:

- Double-check your email and password  
- Ensure your internet connection is stable  
- Refresh the login page  
- Make sure your account is still active  
- Click **Forgot Password** or contact the administrator if your account is locked  

</Kendala>
