## **Creating a New Account**

This section is intended for users who do not yet have a FinTeen account and want to create one.

By following the steps below, you can create your personal account and start managing your finances through the FinTeen platform.

### Preparation Before Registration

Before starting the registration process, make sure you have prepared the following:

- An active and accessible email address  
- A strong password for account security  
- Initial balance information to be used as starting data  
- A stable internet connection  

---

### Steps to Register

Follow these steps to create a new FinTeen account:

1. Open your browser and visit [FinTeen](https://capstone-fin-teen.vercel.app/)  
2. Click **"Don't have an account? Sign up"**  
3. Fill in the registration form with your personal information  
4. Click the **"Sign Up"** button  
5. Open your email inbox and check the OTP code we sent. If not found, check your spam folder  
6. Enter the OTP code on the verification page to activate your account  

---

### After Successful Registration

Once your account is successfully created, you can:

- [Log in](./login) using your email and password  
- Access the FinTeen dashboard  
- Manage your personal finances  
- Update your profile information  
- Change your password if needed  

You are now ready to start using FinTeen to manage your finances more wisely and effectively.

## **Usage Tips**

If you encounter issues during registration, consider the following:

**Account Check**

- Make sure the email has not been previously registered  
- Double-check the information you entered  
- Ensure your email can receive OTP messages  

**System Check**

- Make sure your internet connection is stable  
- Use the latest version of your browser  
- Refresh the page if an error occurs  
- Close and reopen your browser if necessary  
- Restart your device if the system is not responding  

**Additional Solutions**

- Try registering again after a few minutes  
- Check the spam or promotions folder in your email  
- Temporarily disable firewall or VPN if it blocks access  

</Tips>

<Kendala>
## **Troubleshooting**

If you experience issues during the registration process, try the following steps:

- Check your spam or promotions folder if the OTP email has not arrived  
- Use an email address that has not been registered before  
- Ensure your internet connection is stable while filling out the form  
- Refresh the registration page if a technical error occurs  
- Contact the support team if registration still fails or the verification link does not work  

</Kendala>
